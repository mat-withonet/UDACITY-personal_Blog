About Me

    - Name: Rick Sanchez

    - Species: Human

    - Occupation: Scientist

    - Age: 70

I am Rick Sanchez I am one of the two eponymous protagonists from the Adult Swim animated television series Rick and Morty. Created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon, I'm a misanthropic alcoholic scientist inspired by Emmett "Doc" Brown from Back to the Future. I'm Known for reckless, nihilistic behavior and pessimistic personality, I have been very well received. I'm a a sociopathic mad scientist who knows everything in the universe and thus finds life a traumatizing and pointless experience. However, despite being the smartest person in the universe, there have been times where I've come close to being wrong.
